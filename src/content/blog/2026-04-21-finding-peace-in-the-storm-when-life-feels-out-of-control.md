> "Peace I leave with you; my peace I give you. I do not give to you as the world gives. Do not let your hearts be troubled and do not be afraid."  
> — **John 14:27**

Life has a way of throwing curveballs when we least expect them. Bills stack up. Relationships strain. Health scares come out of nowhere. And in those moments, the world offers us temporary fixes - scroll a little more, buy a little more, distract a little more.

But Jesus offers something radically different: a peace that does not depend on circumstances. Not the absence of trouble, but the presence of God in the middle of it.

The peace Christ gives is not fragile. It does not shatter when the news is bad or when the path is unclear. It is a peace rooted in the unshakable truth that the Creator of the universe is holding you.

Today, if you are in a storm, know this: you are not forgotten. You are not alone. The same God who calmed the sea is standing with you right now.

## Taking It Further

Knowing truth is only half the battle. Living it is where the rubber meets the road. So what does this look like in your life this week? It might mean having a hard conversation you've been avoiding. It might mean writing down a promise and reading it every morning before your feet hit the floor. It might mean simply pausing at the end of the day to ask one honest question: Did I walk this out today?

Scripture is a living word — and it changes lives when we put it into practice. Don't let today's reading stay on the page. Let it travel with you into every conversation, every decision, every quiet moment. That's when the gospel stops being information and starts being transformation. And that's the kind of faith worth rocking on your sleeve — literally.
