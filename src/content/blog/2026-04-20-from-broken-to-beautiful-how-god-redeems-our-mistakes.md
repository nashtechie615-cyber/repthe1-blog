> "And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose."  
> — **Romans 8:28**

Your worst chapter is not your whole story. The mistake you cannot stop replaying. The season you are ashamed of. The relationship you ruined. God sees all of it - and He is not walking away.

Redemption is God's specialty. He takes the broken pieces and makes something beautiful. Not in spite of the cracks, but through them. Your scars become your testimony.

Moses was a murderer. David was an adulterer. Peter denied Jesus three times. Paul persecuted Christians. And God used every single one of them to change the world.

Whatever you are carrying today, hand it to Him. He is not interested in your perfection. He is interested in your surrender. And from surrender, He builds something glorious.

## Taking It Further

Knowing truth is only half the battle. Living it is where the rubber meets the road. So what does this look like in your life this week? It might mean having a hard conversation you've been avoiding. It might mean writing down a promise and reading it every morning before your feet hit the floor. It might mean simply pausing at the end of the day to ask one honest question: Did I walk this out today?

Scripture is a living word — and it changes lives when we put it into practice. Don't let today's reading stay on the page. Let it travel with you into every conversation, every decision, every quiet moment. That's when the gospel stops being information and starts being transformation. And that's the kind of faith worth rocking on your sleeve — literally.
