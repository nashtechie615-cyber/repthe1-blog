> "Therefore, I urge you, brothers and sisters, in view of God's mercy, to offer your bodies as a living sacrifice, holy and pleasing to God - this is your true and proper worship."  
> — **Romans 12:1**

Worship is not just what happens between the first and last song on Sunday morning. It is how you live every moment of every day. Your work can be worship. Your parenting can be worship. Even your rest can be worship.

When you do your job with excellence, that is worship. When you choose patience in traffic, that is worship. When you forgive when it hurts, that is worship.

The enemy wants you to compartmentalize your faith - God gets Sunday, the world gets Monday through Saturday. But God wants all of you, all the time.

Make today an act of worship. Not by being perfect, but by being present with God in everything you do.

## Taking It Further

Knowing truth is only half the battle. Living it is where the rubber meets the road. So what does this look like in your life this week? It might mean having a hard conversation you've been avoiding. It might mean writing down a promise and reading it every morning before your feet hit the floor. It might mean simply pausing at the end of the day to ask one honest question: Did I walk this out today?

Scripture is a living word — and it changes lives when we put it into practice. Don't let today's reading stay on the page. Let it travel with you into every conversation, every decision, every quiet moment. That's when the gospel stops being information and starts being transformation. And that's the kind of faith worth rocking on your sleeve — literally.
