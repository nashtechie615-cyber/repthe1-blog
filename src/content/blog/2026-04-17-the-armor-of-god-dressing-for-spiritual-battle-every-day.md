> "Put on the full armor of God, so that you can take your stand against the devil's schemes."  
> — **Ephesians 6:11**

Every morning, you get dressed for the day ahead. But how often do you dress for the spiritual battle? Because whether you see it or not, there is a battle raging for your mind, your heart, and your purpose.

The armor of God is not decorative - it is functional. The belt of truth. The breastplate of righteousness. The shield of faith. The helmet of salvation. The sword of the Spirit.

These are not metaphors to admire - they are weapons to wield. Truth combats lies. Righteousness guards your heart. Faith extinguishes doubt. The Word cuts through deception.

You would not walk into a storm without a coat. Do not walk into your day without your armor.
