> "For we live by faith, not by sight."  
> — **2 Corinthians 5:7**

Faith is believing in what you cannot see. It is trusting the bridge will hold before you see the other side. It is planting a seed and watering dirt for months before anything breaks through.

Our culture worships evidence. Data. Proof. And there is nothing wrong with evidence - but faith begins where certainty ends. It is the substance of things hoped for.

Abraham left his home without knowing where he was going. Moses approached the Red Sea without knowing it would part. Mary said yes without knowing how it would work.

What is God asking you to trust Him with today? Take the step. He has never failed anyone who walked toward Him in faith.
